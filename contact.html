Contact Information

In order to contact me, please use the following email address (replace "_at_" to "@".). Through my email address, you will be able to contact me, and also order original tracks. In order to use tracks that have been published in this website, please consult and inform me through my email.

email: refluster_at_gmail.com

Order

To place an order for the original tracks, please contact me via email. The email should include the following:

  • The individual or group name
  • Contact information
  • Requirements (e.g. genre, mood, etc.)
  • Deadline (2 months later on average)
  • Data format (e.g. wav, mp3, wma, etc.)
  • Production cost (need to discussion)
Computer graphics, jacket design for the original tracks, are also provided on your demand. If you want, please notice in the email.

Terms of service

Before using our original tracks, please notify me via email, the purpose of such use. In some situations, I have allowed the use of the original tracks to be used in radio-shows, websites, and events. However, if the purpose is not in correlated to any of the following, I will see it over, and email the results.

  • Use tracks for business
  • Use tracks included in our CD package
  • Use tracks composed in collaboration with other composers of a different label
