WhiteSeason Broadcast on ETN.FM

An official WhiteSeason radio-show on the Trance Channel at ETN.fm. ETN.fm is one of the worlds Trance radio station. The website of the radio station, along with quick links, of the trance channels are as follows:

Broadcasted programs, such as the WhiteSeason's Mix, are being played every other week. While another week are guest Mix from other music labels. WhiteSeason's Mix introduces a number of brand new tracks, and promos from Japan.

Broadcasts are on every Tuesday. However, from the 3rd Tuesday of March until the 1st week of November, the schedules of the broadcasted programs were advanced by an hour because of daylight savings time. The time table shows as follows:

  • 22:00 - 23:00 (JST)
  • 13:00 - 14:00 (UTC)
  • 05:00 - 06:00 (PST)
