Brief summary

DJ name: refluster
Real name: Koh Uehara
Sex: Male

Activity

As of 2006, he belongs to the music label called "WhiteSeason". He compose music, genre of trance, house and healings, and also creates computer graphics for CD jackets and promotion videos. His original work is available on WhiteSeason's website or on their CDs.

Profile

Born in Kyoto, Japan in 1986. He had developed an interest in trance music and started to compose music, it was the year he entered into a university on 2004. Around the same time, he was invited to WhiteSeason label to launch full-scale productions. From the same year, he has broadcasted his works via ETN.fm, one of the most popular trance music radio stations in the world.

Interest

His favorite music is "Follow you (Mike Shiver's Catching Sun Remix)". He was affected from the work and it made him to be interested in magnificent, emotional and melodious music. He prefer the combination of piano, synthesizer stringth and strong kick. With the instruments, his is able to create different type of sound from the existing trend from Trance, House, Healing music.

Computer graphics is also one of his interests, especially abstract art. In the WhiteSeason, he works as a CD jacket designer/director and promotion video creator.
